Overview

CWS Inventory is a modern inventory and stock management solution built for businesses of all sizes. It helps you streamline inventory operations, reduce manual errors, and make data-driven decisions with powerful analytics.

Real-time Inventory Tracking

Track stock levels in real-time across all locations and warehouses.

Sales & Purchase Management

Manage purchases, sales orders, and returns efficiently

Multi-warehouse Support

Handle multiple warehouses and locations from a single system.

Advanced Reports

Generate detailed reports and analytics for better decision-making

Low Stock Alerts

Get instant alerts for low stock, expiring items, and reorder levels

Role-based Access

Secure your data with role-based permissions and user management.
